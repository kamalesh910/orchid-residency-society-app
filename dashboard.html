Society Rules

1. Spitting and Parking

No spitting in society areas. A fine of ₹200 for offenders. Vehicles must be parked properly with parking stickers. Improper parking will result in the vehicle’s air being let out and a fine of ₹200.

2. Hawkers and Parcel Collection

Hawkers are not allowed (e.g., Swiggy, Zomato). Collect parcels at the gate.

3. Rental Agreements

Rooms must not be rented to boys and girls without a rent agreement. Water supply will be cut off if no agreement is submitted.

4. Water Usage

Water is supplied by tankers. Turn off taps when going out. Fine of ₹650 for running taps.

5. Shop Owners Maintenance

Shop owners must pay full maintenance if using water.

6. Parking and Gates

Do not leave the parking gate open. Action will be taken if left open.

7. Terrace and Laundry

No clothes should be hung to dry on the terrace.

8. Maid Information

Maid's visiting time and Aadhaar card should be submitted to the society.

9. Gate Key

Request a gate key in writing. If the gate is found open at night and theft occurs, the key will be confiscated.

10. Maintenance Charges

Maintenance is ₹1500 (responsible residents) and ₹2500 (non-responsible residents). Fines will be imposed for late payment, and water supply will be cut off for non-payment.

11. Complaints

Any complaints should be submitted in writing and placed in the complaint box.

12. Garbage Disposal

A fine of ₹500 will be imposed if garbage is thrown from the balcony.

13. Tenant Notification

Tenants must notify the society 15 days before vacating. Rent agreement must be submitted when a new tenant moves in.

14. Lift Usage

The lift cannot be used when moving household items.

15. Society Meetings

Meetings are held every Sunday. A fine of ₹50 will be imposed for non-attendance. If the owner is absent, tenants can attend.

16. Security Personnel

No arguments with security personnel. Contact the responsible person for issues.

17. Lift Maintenance

Minor lift issues may take 3 days to repair, while major issues could take longer.
